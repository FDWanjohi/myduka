Steply: A Hiking and Steps log App

Has two sections:
i.	Steps Log
ii.	Hiking Section
About:
i.	Steps Log: Step Counter Section
-	Daily Step Log
-	Steply Monthly Calendar
-	Challenges: 
Users can create private and public challenges based on number of steps
Each Challenge will have a chatroom and leaderboard
Each will have achievement badges 

ii.	Hiking Section: Hosts information about hiking 
-	Weather forecast
-	Safety and precautions
-	Maps & Navigation
-	Discover Trails & Rotes: posted based on difficulty levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Hard, Extremely hard
-	Hing Requirements / Checklist
-	Route planning
-	Search BAR: Connected to AI

iii.	Social Community Center: where users can engage and post, comment.
